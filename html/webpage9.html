Facilities






Library :-

A well equipped and well managed library is the foundation of modern education system. Library has the same position in an educational institution as the heart in a body. It circulates the knowledge and information through body of the college. The College library consists of books of all specialties. National and International journals are also available. Library is spacious with adequate seating arrangements. To get access to modern trends in the field of health care administration, internet facilities are also provided in the college. A qualified librarian is in charge of the library.

Laboratories :-

The college is spacious and has Lecture halls with most modern teaching equipments. Well-equipped AV Aids Lab, and Computer Lab, Nursing Foundation Lab, Anatomy Lab, Nutrition Lab, Community Health Lab, Maternal and Child Health Lab are provided in the college for practical training.

Hostel :-

The College is providing clean and hygienic residential facilities within the campus. Separate hostel is provided for male and female students. Hostel rules have been framed keeping in mind the local culture and traditions with a view to guide and help students to attain standard of implacable conduct.

Transport Facility :-

To ensure safe travel the College has its own fleet of College buses designed as per standards and manned by trained drivers. Mobile phones have been provided in each bus that ensures efficiency in terms of service and better communication in case of emergencies.

Scholarships :-

Scholarships and Scholarship facilitation Services are being provided to the deserving students especially from educationally, economically and socially backward areas.
